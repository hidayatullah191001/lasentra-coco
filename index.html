Our Product

Coconut Sugar

Coconut sugar is made from selected coconut tree sap and processed in Banyumas Regency, Central Java. Coconut sugar is good for health because it has a very low glycemic index (GI of 35) compared to cane sugar, which has a GI of 68 and a GI of 55, making it good for diabetics.
